Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1/21/2025

Overview

CarryFit ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

Cookie Usage

We use two types of cookies on our website:

  • Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to function properly and remember your preferences. They include:
    • User preferences (measurement system, favorite airlines)
    • Feature discovery data (which new features you've seen)
    • Changelog information (last viewed updates)
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website through PostHog analytics. When enabled:
    • Your visits can be tracked between page reloads and future visits on this device
    • Usage patterns help us improve the website experience
    • All data remains anonymous and no personal information is collected

    If analytics cookies are disabled, any tracking data is cleared when you reload or leave the page, ensuring complete anonymity between visits.

Information We Collect

Through PostHog analytics, we may collect the following anonymous usage data:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the website
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Interaction data (e.g., button clicks, form submissions)
  • General location data

This data collection is optional and depends on your cookie preferences.

How We Use Your Information

The collected analytics data is used solely for:

  • Understanding how users interact with our website
  • Improving user experience
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Making data-driven decisions about feature development

Your Privacy Choices

You have full control over your privacy preferences:

  • Choose between necessary-only or analytics cookies through our consent banner
  • Opt out of analytics tracking at any time
  • Change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser data
  • Use browser privacy features or extensions to block analytics scripts
  • No personal identifiable information is ever collected or stored

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
